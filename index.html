Selected Papers

Full list here. *equal contribution.

CLIPSym: Delving into Symmetry Detection with CLIP
Tinghan Yang, Md Ashiqur Rahman, Raymond A. Yeh
ICCV, 2025
paper / code /

We propose CLIPSym, a framework that leverages pre-trained CLIP with our Semantic-Aware Prompt Grouping (SAPG) and a rotation-equivariant decoder to achieve SOTA reflection and rotation symmetry detection across multiple benchmark datasets.

Cliqueparcel: An approach for batching llm prompts that jointly optimizes efficiency and faithfulness
Jiayi Liu, Tinghan Yang, Jennifer Neville
preprint, 2024
arXiv

We propose CliqueParcel, a prompt batching framework that improves LLM inference efficiency while preserving output faithfulness across diverse tasks.

S3: Side-Channel Attack on Stylus Pencil through Sensors
Habiba Farrukh*, Tinghan Yang*, Hanwen Xu, Yuxuan Yin, He Wang, Z. Berkay Celik
Ubicomp, 2021
paper

We present S3, a side-channel attack system that exploits iPad motion sensor data to accurately infer what users write with the Apple Pencil, revealing significant privacy risks.

AirSign: Smartphone Authentication by Signing in the Air
Yubo Shao, Tinghan Yang, He Wang, Jianzhu Ma
Sensors, 2020
paper

We propose AirSign, a smartphone authentication system that leverages built-in acoustic and motion sensors to verify users by signing their signatures in the air, achieving a 97.1% F-score against forgeries.
